The human condition

do you ask the gender first or the age of the killer? The socio-economic class or race? The motive
or the method? The thinking around this drives the separation, the generation of an other, by
allowing the necessary reduction of complexity for anyones capacity for attention. This further extends
into how significant any one thing is: more significant, more boxes one employes.
